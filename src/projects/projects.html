A series of proteomics analysis

Proteomics Analysis

Below, a data model to hold data from a series of proteomics analysis is defined. It contains the following tables:

1. User: scientist leading the proteomic experiment.

2. Experiments: wet-lab experiment.

3. Experimental protocols: experimental tecnhiques related to the wet-lab experiment.

4. Samples: metadata associated to the experiments.

5. Peptide sequences: sequences identified in the proteomic experiment (e.g. results from Mass spectrometry).

6. Proteins: proteins identified after performing a bioinformatic analysis on the proteomic experiment results.

7. References: citations from either experiments or protocols.
